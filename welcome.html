BPHC Library Paper Tracker

Welcome to the BPHC DSpace extension!

Usage

  • Navigate to a BPHC library page where papers are listed, like this page.
  • Click on the dropdown button ("Status") displayed in the table view
  • Click on the current status of the paper.
The progress is saved automatically and will be restored when you visit the paper collection page again.

Viewing at a glance

To view all your saved progress, click on the extension icon in the chrome extensions puzzle icon dropdown (top right of the browser)
